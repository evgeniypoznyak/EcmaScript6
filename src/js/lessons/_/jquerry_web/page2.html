Curious Facts About iPhone

Did you know that iPhone started out as a tablet project? Steve Jobs himself admitted in an interview that his team tried to develop a small computer with a virtual keyboard. As a result, Jobs decided to apply that technology to a cell phone. Therefore the company put the initial project aside and the team went to work on the phone.

The First iPhone Was Designed in 1983

In 1983 Hartmut Esslinger designed a landline phone with iPhone and iPad features. In addition, a stylus controlled its display, but the idea was abandoned.
